Homemade Carne Asada Tacos

Description

Are you looking for a dinner idea that your family will enjoy (including the picky-eaters) and does not require hours on end?

You're on the right page at the right time. Not only are our Homemade Carne Asada Tacos are delicious, you only need four key ingredients! If you have leftover chicken or steak from previous dinners, this recipe just became a whole lot easier.

Ingredients

  • corn tortillas
  • carne asada
  • red onion
  • cilantro
  • sour cream
  • hot sauce

Instructions

  1. grill your carne asada
  2. slice the carne asada into small cubes
  3. toast your corn tortillas
  4. smear sour cream to the tortilla
  5. add the carne asada
  6. add chopped red onions
  7. add freshly chopped cilantro
  8. add hot sauce of your liking
